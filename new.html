About our apartment


'The Address' is a newly refurbished, self contained one bedroom apartment, located in the heart of Carcassonne at the end of a quiet cul de sac offering off street parking directly outside.

The apartment is within easy walking distance of Carcassonne town, The Bastide, between the River Aude and the Canal du Midi

Situated on the ground floor, it has a king size bed, a built- in wardrobe, a modern bathroom with shower with a wash basin and toilet. The living room is open plan with a large sofa, TV (showing French and English channels), DVD player and WIFI. The well equipped kitchen leads to double doors onto a private terrace which has a table and chairs The apartment is attached to the owners property

'The Address' is ideally placed for shopping/restaurants in town, trips along the Canal de Midi and exploring the attractions Carcassonne has to offer, the benefits of being close to town and being in a quiet neighbourhood ......
